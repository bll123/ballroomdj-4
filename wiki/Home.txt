
### BallroomDJ 4

BallroomDJ 4 (BDJ4) is a music player designed for laptops and
desktops.  It is designed to play music for an entire evening without
any intervention.  BDJ4 provides the tools to organize the ballroom
music by dance and into song lists, sequences and automatic playlists.
Though it was designed for the needs of ballroom music, it can be used
for any type of music.
