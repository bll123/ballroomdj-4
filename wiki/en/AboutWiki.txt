## About this Wiki

The images shown in the wiki were taken from BDJ4 running on Linux
using the _Matcha-dark-sea_ theme.  These images may appear different
than what you see on other operating systems with other user interface
themes.

If there is a particular subject you want to see covered or a wiki
page that needs to be improved, please use one of the methods in
[Getting&nbsp;Help](en-Getting%20Help) to send a message to the
development team.

###### Status as of 2022-12

This wiki was started in 2022-12.

Some wiki pages have not been written yet.  Some wiki pages are not
very good yet -- it may take several rewrites before the wiki pages
are fully mature.
