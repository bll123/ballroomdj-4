### Configure : Edit Genres

If you want to use genres, you will also want to change the
[Display&nbsp;Settings](en-Configuration-Display%20Settings).

The __Classical?__ selection is used for classical music genres.  The
classical music setting affects the right-click action in song
selection listings.

To add a new genre, select the __Add&nbsp;New__&nbsp;(![Add
New](https://ballroomdj4.sourceforge.io/wikiimg/AddNewButton-E.png))
button.  Click on the name to change the name.

Select the
__Delete__&nbsp;(![Delete](https://ballroomdj4.sourceforge.io/wikiimg/DeleteButton-E.png))
button to delete a genre.

Use the up and down
arrows&nbsp;(![move-up](https://ballroomdj4.sourceforge.io/wikiimg/UpButton-B.png),&nbsp;![move-down](https://ballroomdj4.sourceforge.io/wikiimg/DownButton-B.png))
to change the order of the genres to match your preference.
