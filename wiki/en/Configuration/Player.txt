### Configuration : Player

<span style="color:#4559bf;">_Basic configuration for the player._</span>

The __Player__ and __Audio__ settings generally do not change.

___Audio Output___: Selects which audio output device will be used to
play the audio.  If the selected device is not currently connected,
the default device will be used.

___Default Volume___: Sets the volume level when BDJ4 first starts.

___Queue Length___: The number of songs the music queue is filled with.

___Completion Message___: A message displayed on the marquee when the dance
is finished (no more songs left to play).
