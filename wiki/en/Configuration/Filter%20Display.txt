### Configuration : Filter Display

The song selection filter has several filters that are not normally
displayed.  These filters can be turned on here.

The standard filters that are always displayed are _Sort&nbsp;By_,
_Search_, _Dance_, and _Dance&nbsp;Rating_.

The additional filters that can be selected are _Genre_,
_Dance&nbsp;Level_, _Status_, _Favorite_, and _Playable&nbsp;Status_.
