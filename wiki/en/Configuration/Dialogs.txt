### Configure : Dialogs

The fields displayed in the song selection filter dialog and the quick
edit dialog may be configured here.

#### Filter Songs

The song selection filter has several filters that are not normally
displayed.  These filters can be turned on here.

The standard filters that are always displayed are _Sort&nbsp;By_ and
_Search_.

The _Dance_, and _Dance&nbsp;Rating_ filter selections are enabled by
default.

Additional filters that may be selected are _Genre_,
_Dance&nbsp;Level_, _Status_, _Favorite_, and _Playable&nbsp;Status_.

#### Quick Edit

The quick edit dialog allows the following fields:
_Speed&nbsp;Adjustment_, _Volume&nbsp;Adjustment_, _Dance&nbsp;Level_,
_Dance&nbsp;Rating_, and _Favorite_.

By default, all fields are displayed.  Select the fields that you want
displayed.
