### Getting Help

If you need any help with installation, how to get started, or how to
use BallroomDJ 4, you can use any of the following options:

__SourceForge Tickets__: A ticket can be opened at SourceForge.  This
requires a SourceForge account.
[Register at SourceForge](https://sourceforge.net/user/registration)
[View Current Tickets](https://sourceforge.net/p/ballroomdj4/tickets/)
[Create New Ticket](https://sourceforge.net/p/ballroomdj4/tickets/new/)

__BallroomDJ 4 Forums__: The BallroomDJ 4 forums also require
registration.
[BDJ4 Problem Reports](https://ballroomdj.org/forum/viewforum.php?f=29)

__BDJ4 Built-In Support__: If you have BDJ4 installed, you can use the
built-in support function.  Start BDJ4, select the __Support__ button,
select __Send Support Message__.  Enter your e-mail address, the
subject and the message you want to send.  You can optionally attach
your data files and/or your database.  Select the __Send Support
Message__ button when finished.
