### Data Privacy

BallroomDJ&nbsp;4 (BDJ4) registers the software upon installation or
upgrade.  If the computer is not connected to the internet during
the installation, no registration information will be sent.

The registration information includes your IP address (so that the
user's country can be determined), your user account name and computer
name.  Any other registration information is related to the BDJ4
version and operating system.

At all other times, BDJ4 never sends any telemetry or other
information.
