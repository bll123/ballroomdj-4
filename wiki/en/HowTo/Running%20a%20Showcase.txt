### How To : Running a Showcase

##### Configuring a Music Queue for a Showcase

For the showcase music queue, in
__Configure&nbsp;/&nbsp;Music&nbsp;Queues__, configure a music queue
with the __Gap&nbsp;Between&nbsp;Songs__ set to 0.0, and no fade-out
time. Make sure __Play&nbsp;Announcements__ is off.  Turn
__Pause&nbsp;Each&nbsp;Song__ on. The name of the music queue is set
to _Showcase_.

Close the configuration window to save the configuration changes.
